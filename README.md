# timer

Simple countdown timer for small people

* Initial Version
    5 colored buttons, with preset countdown times (5 or 10 seconds) and end sounds:
      - red: 5 seconds, 
      - orange: 5 seconds, 
      - yellow: 5 seconds, Cow (moo.wav)
      - green: 10 seconds
      - blue: 10 seconds
